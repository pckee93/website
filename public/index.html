Poh Choo Kee

Originally from Malaysia, Poh has been playing and performing music since she was 3 years old, which eventually led her into discovering her love for entertainment and arts. She started acting when she was 19, starting off with theatre and slowly moving on to commercials, TV and film. She has been consistently training with the top industry professionals in acting, including Joe Hasham (OAM) - who was awarded the Medal of the Order of Australia - and is consistently praised for her commitment and determination in becoming a professional working actor.

Currently based in Melbourne, Australia, Poh is a full time actor and model, landing roles in Australian TV and short film. She is currently writing and producing theatre shows for festivals in Melbourne, and some of her devised pieces have been showcased, including La Mama theatre and LA's famous immersive experience that premiered in Melbourne in late 2017.
